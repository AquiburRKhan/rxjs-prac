Of Observable:

Description:

Converts the arguments to an observable sequence. Unlike from, it does not do any flattening and emits each argument in whole as a separate next notification.

Examples 1:

of ( 10, 20, 30 )
.subscribe() --->
next: 10
next: 20
next: 30

Examples 2:

of ( [ 10, 20, 30 ] )
.subscribe() ---> next: (3)Â [10, 20, 30]
Returns full array

Examples 3:

of ('Hello World')
.subscribe() --->
next: Hello World
