From Observable:

Description:

Creates an Observable from an Array, an array-like object, a Promise, an iterable object, or an Observable-like object. A String, in this context, is treated as an array of characters.

Examples 1:

from ( 10, 20, 30 )
.subscribe() --->
ERROR TypeError: number is not observable

Examples 2:

from ( [ 10, 20, 30 ] )
.subscribe() --->
next: 10
next: 20
next: 30

Examples 3:

from ('Hello World')
.subscribe() --->
next: H
next: e
next: l
next: l
next: o
next:
next: W
next: o
next: r
next: l
next: d
